Information for OEMs

Bliss OS brings the best of what Android has to offer to your customers.

With a wide variety of compatibility for devices built in, we can help to bring the experience your customers demand, and the features they deserve.

Learn More

Partners & Retailers

We offer two ways for OEMs to include our work, with differing requirements and benefits.

Partners

One of the ways we work with our partners is through innovation, and we try to help spread that love by offering OEMs and Engineers builds to test

Requirements:  
- Official involvement from BlissLabs. 
- Negotiable per-device royalty Strict software guidelines 
- Trademark compliance

Retailers

Have a device that comes with Bliss already? We have a few guidelines that we need to follow first.

Requirements: 
- Selling devices with Bliss or Bliss OS
- Per-device royalty encouraged 
- Trademark compliance

Resources

We offer OEM toolkits, custom options for automatic installation, as well as endless other customization options. 

Installation

We recommend using our licensed OEM installation toolkit, but we also provide other automated install options. With these tools,  OEMs can install the OS, configure anything necessary, and then prepare the device for shipping to end users.

Customization

We allow our partners a level of added customization options for branding and themes. These are all offered through our partner licensing agreements and allow your customers to reatin their identity and promote their brand.

System Settings

We work with you for specific device tuning and in many cases offer assistance to achieve the targets you require.

OTA Updates & LTS Contracts

We offer OTA updates for a variety of hardware and offer Long Term Service contracts as well. 

Visual Branding Guide

We offer a loose branding guide for our partners to follow. Feel free to contact us with any questions you may have

Branding Guide

Third-Party Repositories & Packages

We recommend all software included in Bliss OS releases be inspected and agreed upon through official involvement with BlissLabs in order to ensure user privacy and security guidelnes. All third-party software must also agree to present source code for us to inspect as well. Failure to do this may void any support contracts or retail contracts.
